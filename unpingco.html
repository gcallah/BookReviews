This book needed better editing, to avoid sentences like, "The pip installer does not check for such conflicts checks only if the proposed package already has its dependencies installed and will install them if not or remove existing incompatible modules" (3) or "Visual Studio Community Edition... usually has enough to compile many C-codes" (3). The author recommends dividing by "the fractional powers of 1/2" (14) when he means "the powers of the fraction 1/2." Later he says an increase in dimensionality would necessitate "1000 more data points" instead of "1000 times more data points" (218). Of course these are just careless errors -- surely the author meant to write the latter version in both cases -- but such errors can seriously hamper a novice trying to master a new subject.
