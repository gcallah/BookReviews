Peter Lamb. Socialism. Cambridge ; Medford, Massachusetts : Polity Press, 2019. 135 pages.

Lamb, for instance, notes Sarkar’s claim that “many people in capitalist developing countries could only dream of the economic living standards of the German democratic republic” (5). True, acrylic my reply, but the east German workers we’re enjoying a standard of living that had declined from Germany’s capitalist past, while those in developing countries were experiencing rising standards of living as their nations had become more capitalist. The critics claim may or may not be true: the point I wish to make here is that Lamb typically does not even consider such opposing points of view to his.
